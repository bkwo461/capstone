Questions About Us

How can I get started with Cloud Engine and explore its capabilities?

We offer a comprehensive documentation that meticulously guides you through the nuances of our website and services.
This invaluable resource is designed to expedite your onboarding process, ensuring you can quickly leverage the power of Cloud Engine.

What is Cloud Engine, and what does it aim to achieve?

Cloud Engine aims to simplify cloud technology selection and management by intelligently recommending
solutions based on user requirements. We offer a unified interface for seamless provider switching while ensuring data portability.

Can you tell us more about the Green Computing project and its role in Cloud Engine's development?

The Green Computing project within the School of Computer Science is a key partner in Cloud Engine's development,
providing expertise to enhance the eco-friendliness of our cloud services.
